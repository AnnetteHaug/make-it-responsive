Frugal Innovation. Be simple, Not complicated!

Inova is a newly formed company. We want to share our experience of frugal innovation. We are now hosting innovation camps anyone can join.


What is frugal innovation?

Frugal innovation or frugal engineering is the process of reducing the complexity and cost of a good and its production.

Welcome to
Inoovation Camp

The Innovation Camp is a Winter camp organised by Inova. Throughout the camp, the participants will experience an intense innovation process. Inova have partnered up with Oslo metropolitan university to establish a unique environment.

This is the first year Inova hosts an Innovation Camp, where more than 350 students can participated from across Norway. The Innovation Camp has received recognition for its approach to frugal innovation. The participated will team up in groups, which they in five days have to develop into a working prototype. The students wrap up the course by delivering a presentation in front of guests and Inova management.
